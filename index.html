About Us

Narumugai Foundation is dedicated to promoting the well-being of children from separated families. We believe every child deserves the love and care of both parents, regardless of their marital status.

Our Focus Areas
  • Child Welfare
  • Shared Parenting
  • Family Support
  • Advocacy

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To promote and facilitate shared parenting arrangements that ensure children maintain meaningful relationships with both parents after separation.

Our Vision

A world where every child receives unconditional love and support from both parents, creating emotionally healthy and well-adjusted individuals.
